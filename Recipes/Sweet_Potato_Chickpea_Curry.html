Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

Description

This sweet potato chickpea curry is a yummy vegan dish.

Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Season with garam masala, cumin, turmeric, salt, and chile flakes. Add spinach right before serving.
